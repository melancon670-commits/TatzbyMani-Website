About Mani

Born in Oakland and raised in Sacramento, Mani turned her artistic passion into a professional career beginning September 13th, 2023 She focuses on precision, cleanliness, and creating tattoos that represent identity and story with a luxury client experience she is proud to offer. Mani specializes in fine line, blackwork, and lettering tattoos, but is open to all styles and custom requests. With a commitment to quality and client satisfaction, Mani ensures every tattoo is a unique work of art that clients will cherish forever.
